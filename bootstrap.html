Bootstrapping


Bootstrap Your Controllers

Once you have your domains built, you can then 'autogenerate' your controllers using the following command:

./gradlew GenerateController

This will generate the controllers for all your domains using basic CRUD functionality. NOTE:There is no need to add annotations or anything to them as this is all handled by the BeAPI framework.


Bootstrap Your IO State

After you have built your domains AND controllers, you can then 'autogenerate your IO State files using the following command:

./gradlew GenerateIostate

This will then create all the necessary state files for your endpoints used by the framework.
